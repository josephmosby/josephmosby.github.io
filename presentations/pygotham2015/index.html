Our ALU

Inputs: x, y - two 16-bit binary numbers
Control Bits: zx, nx, zy, ny, f, no
Outputs:
x + yx - yy - x
01-1
xy-x
-y!x!y
x + 1y + 1x - 1
y - 1x & yx | y

Outputs: zr if output == 0, ng if output < 0

Our Parts

  • Single-bit OR
  • Single-bit NOT
  • Single-bit AND
  • 16-bit Multiplexer
  • 16-bit bitwise NOT
  • 16-bit bitwise AND
  • A 16-bit incrementer
