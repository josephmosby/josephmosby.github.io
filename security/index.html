projects in security

General Computer Controls I led a team of security auditors through access control and security logging process walkthrough exercises as part of an enterprise Sarbanes-Oxley audit readiness exercise. In this role, my team and I performed initial testing of the client's internal controls, documented the client's internal processes, and suggested process and control improvements to enhance the client's security environment.
PCI-DSS I served a large quasi-government agency in their efforts to obtain PCI-DSS Level 1 certification, the standard security certification for credit card processors. My team was engaged to develop strategy and process to prepare our client and its substantially large IT environment for a clean PCI-DSS audit. In this role, I managed the agency's change procedures as they worked to create a stable, secure credit card data environment.
